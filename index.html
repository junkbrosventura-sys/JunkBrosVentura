About Junk Bros

Junk Bros is a locally owned and operated junk removal business based in Ventura, California. Founded and run by two motivated local teens, we provide fast, affordable, and reliable junk removal services for homeowners, renters, and small businesses. We specialize in residential cleanouts, furniture and appliance removal, yard waste hauling, and move-out junk removal. Our goal is to make clearing out unwanted items simple, stress-free, and accessible while giving back to the Ventura community through honest work and dependable service.
